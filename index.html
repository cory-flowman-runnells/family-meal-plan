Garlic Chicken Pasta

⏱ 12 min ▼

Ingredients (2 adults)

  • 12 oz whole wheat pasta
  • 1.5 lbs chicken breast, sliced thin
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 large handfuls baby spinach
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • Black pepper & oregano to taste

Steps

  1. Boil pasta per package. Before draining, scoop out kids' portion and toss with butter. Set aside.
  2. While pasta cooks, heat 1 tbsp olive oil in a pan over medium-high. Cook chicken 3–4 min per side until done. Slice.
  3. In the same pan, add 2 tbsp olive oil and garlic. Cook 60 seconds.
  4. Add drained pasta and spinach to the pan. Toss until spinach wilts (1 min).
  5. Add chicken back in. Squeeze lemon over everything. Plate and serve.
Sodium tip: Skip the salt entirely. Garlic + lemon + olive oil provides all the flavor you need. Taste before you reach for the shaker.

Turkey Taco Bowls

⏱ 15 min ▼

Ingredients (2 adults)

  • 1.5 lbs ground turkey
  • 2 cups instant brown rice
  • 1 can black beans, drained & rinsed
  • 2 avocados, diced
  • 1 large tomato, diced
  • Plain Greek yogurt (instead of sour cream)
  • 1 tsp cumin, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp paprika

Steps

  1. Cook instant brown rice per package (usually 90 seconds microwave).
  2. Brown turkey in a pan over medium-high heat, breaking it up, 6–7 min.
  3. Add cumin, garlic powder, and paprika. Stir to coat. Cook 1 more min.
  4. Pull out plain turkey + rice for kids before next steps.
  5. Warm the rinsed black beans briefly in the same pan.
  6. Build bowls: rice, turkey, beans, tomato, avocado. Top with Greek yogurt.
Skip the packet: Store-bought taco seasoning packets can have 300–400mg sodium per serving. The homemade spice mix above has zero.

Sheet Pan Chicken & Veggies

⏱ 10 min prep ▼

Ingredients (2 adults)

  • 1.5 lbs chicken breast
  • 2 cups broccoli florets (frozen OK)
  • 2 medium carrots, sliced
  • 1 large sweet potato, cubed
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp oregano or Italian seasoning
  • Black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. On a sheet pan, toss veggies and chicken with olive oil, garlic powder, oregano, and pepper.
  3. Spread everything out in a single layer.
  4. Roast 22–25 min until chicken is cooked through and edges are golden.
  5. On a second pan: kids' nuggets go in at the same time. Mac & cheese on stovetop.
Hands-off dinner: 10 minutes to prep, then the oven handles it. Perfect night to help with homework or just decompress.

Black Bean Quesadillas

⏱ 10 min ▼

Ingredients (2 adults)

  • 4 whole wheat tortillas
  • 1 can black beans, drained & rinsed
  • 2 big handfuls spinach
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • ½ cup low-fat shredded cheese
  • 2 avocados
  • Plain Greek yogurt
  • Olive oil for the pan

Steps

  1. Make kids' cheese quesadillas first (plain tortilla + cheese, 2 min per side). Set aside.
  2. Layer beans, spinach, tomato & cheese on one tortilla. Fold in half.
  3. Cook in a little olive oil over medium heat, 2–3 min per side until golden.
  4. Slice and serve topped with diced avocado and a dollop of Greek yogurt.
Rinse those beans: Running canned black beans under cold water for 10 seconds removes a significant portion of the added sodium. Worth the 10 seconds.

Chicken Stir-Fry over Rice

⏱ 15 min ▼

Ingredients (2 adults)

  • 1.5 lbs chicken breast, sliced thin
  • 2 cups instant brown rice
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 2 medium carrots, sliced thin
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1–2 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce

Steps

  1. Cook instant brown rice (microwave bag, 90 sec).
  2. Heat oil in a large pan or wok over high heat. Cook chicken 4–5 min until done. Pull out kids' portion now.
  3. Add garlic to the pan — 30 seconds.
  4. Add broccoli, carrots, bell pepper. Stir-fry 4–5 min until tender-crisp.
  5. Add chicken back in. Drizzle with low-sodium soy sauce. Toss and serve over rice.
Make extra: This reheats perfectly. Double the adult portion and tomorrow's lunch is already handled.
